Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat ‚ÄúGarlicky & Peppery‚Äù Yoshinoya Beef Bowl / Gyudon / Gyumeshi:

  1. 500 gr US Shortplate Sliced Beef
  2. 1/2 tea spoon salt
  3. 1/2 tea spoon medium grind black pepper (lada hitam kasar, for bombing taste)
  4. 4 table spoon worcestershire sauce (kecap inggris)
  5. 4 table spoon sesame oil (minyak wijen)
  6. 1 pc onion, slice into ring/dice shape as you wish (bawang bombay, potong dalam bentuk sesuai selera)
  7. 5 cloves garlic, slice (iris tipis karena ini bakal ditumis sampai agak kering, ini bomb banget)
  8. 1/2 tea spoon brown sugar
  9. 2 table spoon butter

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat ‚ÄúGarlicky & Peppery‚Äù Yoshinoya Beef Bowl / Gyudon / Gyumeshi

1
In a bowl, mix beef with black pepper, brown sugar, salt, worcestershire sauce and sesame oil, let it marinatedfor minimum 10 mins
2
When your meat is ready, heat the pan and add the butter/cooking oil
3
Add sliced garlic and stir fry until it turns golden brown and looks crispy
4
Add sliced onion and marinated beef, stir until cooked
5
Serve with warm rice/shirataki rice... For additional bombing taste, you can add sliced chili/chili powder
6
